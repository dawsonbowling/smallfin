Monthly Rate of Return

This rate applies to all investors. Changing it here takes effect for future interest applications only — past transactions are never altered.

Example: 5 = 5% per month. A $100 deposit earns $5 the first month, then compounds on $105 the next month, etc.

How Interest Works

Interest is applied automatically on the 1st of each month based on each investor's deposit history. Deposits made mid-month are prorated by day — e.g. a deposit on the 15th of a 30-day month earns 15/30 of that month's interest.


Changing the rate here applies to all future calculations. You can delete individual deposits from each investor's transaction history and interest will recalculate instantly.
